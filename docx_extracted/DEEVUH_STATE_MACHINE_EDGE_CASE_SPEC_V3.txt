9. State Transition Rules
Orders cannot move to DELIVERED before SHIPPED
Payments must be SUCCESS before order confirmation
Coupons cannot apply after checkout completion
Cancelled orders cannot transition to PROCESSING
Abandoned carts cannot directly become DELIVERED
10. Final Operational Philosophy
The lifecycle and state machine architecture prioritizes operational reliability, payment consistency, and clean admin workflows while remaining lightweight enough for an MVP-stage fashion e-commerce platform.
